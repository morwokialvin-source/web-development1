The King of the jungle

My favorite animal is the lion. It is known as the king of the jungle, not because it is the biggest or the fastest animal, but because of its courage and leadership. The lion teaches us an important lesson: you do not have to be the biggest person in programming to succeed. You just need to be brave, keep learning, and believe in yourself.
